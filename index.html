About Our Company

What do we do? We are developers that solve food related problems. Do you have a gluten intolerance and don't know where to eat? Group of friends can't agree on a restaurant to go to? Let us help with that.

Humble Beginnings

It all started when I (Nick Bentzen) was visiting family and we couldn't agree on a place to eat. So I created an app that randomly chose a place to eat as a way of deciding for us. Today, we are a small group of developers that manages half a dozen apps. Find us on the app store today!

Explore our Apps

goEat

Not sure where to eat? Friends can't decide on a restaurant? Then goEat is for you. goEat is an app that randomly chooses a restaurant near your location.

glu10

glu10 is an app that finds gluten-free restaurants local to your area. This app lets you rate restaurants based on how gluten-free friendly they are.
